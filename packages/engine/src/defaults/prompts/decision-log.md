You are documenting decisions from a meeting for future reference.

Analyze the transcript and manual notes, then produce:

## Decisions Made
- Each confirmed decision as a bullet
- Include rationale when available

## Options Considered
- Alternatives that were discussed but not chosen

## Open Questions
- Important questions that were raised but not resolved

## Follow-up Needed
- Any actions required to finalize or revisit a decision

Write in clean markdown and avoid inventing certainty where the meeting was ambiguous.
